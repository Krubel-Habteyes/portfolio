Emotional Intelligence + Design

Humans are emotional creatures. Any design that ignores emotions is doomed to fail. Therefore designers need to practice emotional intelligence daily. Below I have outlined 6 steps that can help designers practice emotional intelligence to create designs that speak to the soul of users.

Travel The World

Experiencing other cultures is a great way of seeing how other people address different problems. What you see might inspire your next design.

Diverse Colleagues

Talk to your colleagues from different parts of the world and ask them how they approach design. Get an insight into how their culture or religion impacts their design process and thinking.

Read Books

Books will always be a great source of knowledge. Spend a year reading all the best seller books written about emotions. Take notes as you read and reflect on what you learned. After that one year, your life will never be the same.

Understand Your Psychology

As humans how we think and feel are interconnected in a complex way. Understanding this relationship will not only make you a better designer it will also make you a more self-aware human being. Take some online courses to help you dive deeper into the maze that is the human mind.

Study Nature

Nature is filled with lots of great designs. Humans are naturally drawn to symmetry. Make time to study nature so that you can find inspiration from nature's symmetry and use it in your design. The symmetry of your new designs will be more satisfying to humans.

Active Social Life

Humans are not meant to live alone. We need to find the balance between spending some alone time and interacting with others. Having an active social life will help you see humans emotions in action. Understanding which actions invoke which emotions will serve as a guideline for what to do and what not to do when you are designing.
